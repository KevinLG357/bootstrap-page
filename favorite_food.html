Hamburger

To vary the daily menu, it is a good idea to make easy and delicious homemade burgers. With a delicious taste delight the palate of every member of the family.

Hamburger

Ingredients:

  • Hamburger bun with sesame
  • Meat
  • Garlic powder, pepper (for meat) salt
  • Cheddar or Gouda
  • Lettuce
  • Large tomato
  • Onion (optional)
  • Pickles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

How to prepare meat:

In a bowl we place the meat, which will season with salt, garlic powder and pepper to taste, leave in the refrigerator about 25 minutes before using.

Last 25 minutes we will make balls with the meat carefully and we fry crushing or will roast over low heat, until they are desired doneness.

How to prepare burger:

Then we will warm the bread in the pan for a more delicious flavor, followed to this will place the meat.

Now we add a special sauce consisting of mayonnaise and mustard mix evenly, stir to taste and place it above our flesh.

The following will be put Gouda or cheddar cheese to taste, also add one or two leaves of fresh lettuce (previously washed) and a slice of tomato, a slice of onion and pickles.

Finally add the top of the bread and we have our burger finished.

Now comes the most important and fun part, eat! Repeat the process again and again why make one, when you can make two or three?

Sandwich

A sandwich with cheese, tomato, lettuce and cucumber is a excellent idea to start the day, in it we can find a great variety of vitamins and proteins because is a very balanced breakfast.

Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • One or two tomatoes sliced
  • Onion thinly sliced
  • A cucumber thinly sliced
  • White cheese sliced into strips
  • One teaspoon of vinegar or lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt
  • Loaf of bread

Preparation:

First we will to slice all the ingredients, in this case the ingredients are: tomato, cheese, cucumber and if we want add onion but this is optional

We should squeeze lemon and we go to use a teaspoon of its juice, if we prefer we can use a teaspoon of vinegar.

Above of the loaf lower part we will put one lettuce row, later we will put the cheese and continue with the tomato about the cheese, if we want add cucumber and onion we will do it about the tomato, immediately we pour the lemon juice teaspoon or vinegar according to we preference, we will add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor, and we finish covering our sandwich with the loaf top.

Pepperoni pizza

Pepperoni pizza originated in North America. It is a pizza with a base of tomato sauce, mozzarella and pepperoni.

Pepperoni pizza

Ingredients:

  • 100 gr. pepperoni
  • 100 gr. of salami
  • 200 gr. grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 can of crushed tomato
  • 100 gr. grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 homemade pizza dough base
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Basil (optional)

Preparation:

First, Start by preparing the homemade sauce for your pepperoni pizza. Add olive oil to a frying pan and when it is hot add the tomato with salt, pepper and oregano to taste, if desired add some fresh basil leaves for more flavor.

Stir and cook over very low heat until the sauce thickens. If the acidity of the tomato stands out too much, correct it with a pinch of sugar. When the sauce is almost ready, turn the oven on at 200 ºC.

Roll out the homemade dough you have prepared and place it on the tray covered with parchment paper. Prick the dough with a fork so that it does not rise. Then cut the pepperoni and salami into thin slices.

Cover the base with the sauce you have prepared and layer with grated mozzarella cheese, pepperoni and salami slices. Top with Parmesan cheese to taste and bake in the oven for 15 minutes or until perfectly browned.

Wings BBQ

Baked chicken wings with homemade bbq sauce. Ready to prepare at any time of the day. Ideal for sharing with friends and family.

Wings BBQ

Ingredients:

  • 800 gr Chicken wings
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1/2 cup Ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Piquín chili powder
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 60 gr Butter
  • 1 pinch Pepper

Preparation:

Dissolve the salt and sugar in the water. Add vinegar, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, mustard and chili powder. Set aside. Cook garlic and onion in butter.

Add the sugar mixture, season with pepper and cook on the stove for 10 minutes. Stir constantly to prevent sticking.

Cool the sauce, mix with the wings and let stand for half an hour.

Broil the wings for 15 minutes on the grill or until cooked.

Meatballs

A meatball is a ball of minced meat, usually beef and sometimes fish, mixed with eggs and various seasonings, is a popular culinary preparation in different cuisines around the world.

Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 chipotle chile
  • 3 cups of water
  • 2 cubes of Tomato Concentrate with Chicken
  • 1 Tbsp Corn Oil
  • 800 Grams of ground beef
  • 100 Grams of chopped and fried Bacon
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tablespoons Maggi Juice
  • 1 tablespoon of English type Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of finely chopped onion

Preparation:

For the sauce, blend the tomato puree, chili, water and Tomato Concentrate with Chicken. Heat the oil in a saucepan and cook the sauce over medium heat for 10 minutes.

For the meatballs, mix the meat with the bacon, onion, egg, Maggi Juice and Salsa Inglesa. Form the meatballs, place them in the sauce, cover and cook for 20 minutes over medium heat or until cooked.

Serve the meatballs on a plate.

Morisqueta

Stew made with white rice cooked in water with or without salt, similar in texture to steamed rice, as it is not fried. To accompany any dish or can be combined with beans and pork.

Morisqueta

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 Pork Backbone
  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • 15 Ancho Guajillo Chiles
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 of green beans
  • 6 Baby Carrots
  • Thyme and Marjoram

Preparation:

Slice the carrots in big pieces, and the green beans cut them in half.In a pot with approximately four liters of water, add the backbone, carrots, green beans and salt, and if there is another vegetable of your preference, you can also use it.

Place the pot of the spinazo with the vegetables on the stove and let it cook, meanwhile wash the rice, remember that it is completely clean when the water comes out transparent.When the rice is well washed place it in a pot with the four cups of water, add the garlic and salt and let it cook until it is very soft and the water is consumed.

In the meantime, soak the Read...ded guajillo chiles in water, bring to a boil and remove from the stove.

Once the meat is well cooked, blend the guajillo chiles with half the onion and two cloves of garlic, plus salt. Add the blended chiles using a strainer. You can use the water where the chiles were boiled to remove all the chiles from the blender cup.

Then add the thyme and marjoram to the spinazo, let it boil for a few more minutes so that all the flavors are incorporated, then remove the fat from the broth.

Finally, serve the spinazo broth with the morisqueta, you will love it.
